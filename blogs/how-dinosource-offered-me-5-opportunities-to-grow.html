How Dinosource offered me 5 opportunities to grow

Dinosource helps students to establish their personal skills by providing them with opportunities to take on new challenges and responsibilities. In a team setting, members are given the chance to lead projects or take on tasks that are outside of their comfort zone. This can help them develop their problem-solving skills, as well as their ability to think on their feet and handle pressure.

Dinosource?

Dinosource is a design and development agency run by students who create digital experiences for brands. As young, ambitious individuals with a passion for design and development, these students bring a fresh perspective and energy to their work. They are able to think outside the box and come up with innovative solutions that are engaging and effective.

Why I joined the gang

Dinosource partners with companies and organisations, which means that members get the chance to work on projects that are relevant and meaningful . I considered this a great way to gain practical experience and develop my skills in a real-world environment.

The real deal

While working with real clients, we are exposed to challenges and expectations that we often do not encounter in a traditional classroom environment. This can lead us to see the relevance of our courses and understand how our education can be used to make a difference in the world. Moreover, it can help us build our professional network.

Sharpened development skills

In which areas am I growing?

Personally, I notice that my design and development skills have been strengthened by this experience. In addition, it is clear that I work in a more structured way. But the biggest improvement I see is in my communication and feedback skills, which have become much stronger.

how I experience growth

Meanwhile, this is my 4th month at Dinosource and I notice that I'm flourishing. It is a gradual but strong maturation that I notice from myself. I feel I am becoming more self-assured and I am standing more firmly in my shoes.

variety is key

The tasks are tremendously varied, which is very important. Nobody wants a monotonous job with the same assignments every day. You hardly learn anything from that. The more variety in your tasks, the more confirmation you get that you are actually contributing something.

Why you should join Dinosource

Overall, my time in Dinosource has been incredibly valuable for my personal growth. By collaborating with others and taking on new challenges, I have gained valuable experience and developed new skills that will serve me well in my future career. If I can do it, surely you can too!
